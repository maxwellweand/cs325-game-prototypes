Marshmallow Delivers

  • Originality: Have you ever wondered what it's like to live life as a Starship delivery robot? With this game, I have invented a completely accurate simulation of what it's like to deliver goods to consumers that would be better off going to the store and getting it themselves. Never before has technology allowed us to experience GMU campus at its fullest and most fantastical. Join Marshmallow on his journey through campus to find the only glasses of water around - the only glasses that can rehydrate the mariachi band - the only mariachi band that drown out the noise from constant construction. I have never seen this dehydrated mariachi band band on campus, but I dream of it every time I see the ruins of Robinson A. Because they only appear in my dreams, and my dreams are extremely original, it follows that this game based around them is also original. Also, if anyone playing this is interested in writing a fanfiction, they are dehydrated due to fibrotic lung disease, not overplaying. This is also an original reason.
  • Technical Merit: This game has the greatest number of assets of any game I've made so far, by a long shot. My limiting factor in adding mechanics (of which several more were planned) was certainly in the time it takes to make all the art assets. Tilemaps are loaded from a painstakingly created json file, and the map itself is a recreation of GMU campus surrounding the JC. Key items and objectives had to be tracked independently.
